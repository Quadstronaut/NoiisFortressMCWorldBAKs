## 💬 A Message from Noii

Hey everyone 👋

First off — **thank you.** Thank you for every block placed, every build crafted, every raid survived, and every late-night session spent on the Fortress. This community meant the world to me, and I know this news is tough.

**The Quadstronix Minecraft servers are officially shutting down.** 😔

I'm genuinely sorry for the inconvenience and the disappointment. I know many of you poured countless hours into your builds, your farms, your bases — and none of that effort is lost on me. You all made this server something special, and I'll never forget that.

### 🗂️ Your Worlds Are Safe

This repo contains **full world backups** from the Quadstronix servers. Download them, load them up in singleplayer, host them yourself — these builds are yours. You earned them.

> **How to use:** Download the world folder(s) and place them in your Minecraft `saves` directory:
> - **Windows:** `%appdata%\.minecraft\saves\`
> - **macOS:** `~/Library/Application Support/minecraft/saves/`
> - **Linux:** `~/.minecraft/saves/`
